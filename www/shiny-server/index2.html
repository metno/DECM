News : Beta version of DECM application for visualising and exploring climate model data.

To evaluate climate models on a global and regional scales, we propose a straightforward comparison of climate models' ability to reproduce statistical properties such as the seasonal cycle, based on large multi-model ensembles climate model simulations and the predefined IPCC regions. The DECM app contains figures and tables showing examples of simulated vs observed-based datasets of CMIP5 and EURO-CORDEX simulations for various regions and domains

The functions are categorised in two main groups such as 'Product Users' and 'Data Users'. The 'Product users' group focuses on fancy and interactive graphics of outputs, while the 'Data Users' group is more devoted to output data tables.

1. Select your region

E.g. Selected region is Finland

2. Modify few Settings

E.g. Selection of the full multi-model ensemble of simulations for the period 1981-2010

3. Visualize the results

E.g. Seasonal cycle of temperature. Un-corrected EURO-CORDEX simulations for the period 1981-2010 over Finland.

You can open each box and modify parameters that fit your needs - just have a look at our demo videos.

You can use the following embedded DECM App or open a full-screen version which works for all devices i.e Desktops, Tablets, Mobile phones, etc. For mobile phones, we recommend using the Mobile-version, however, some of the figures or tables could be very small or not visible.
